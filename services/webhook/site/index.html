Personal library for your saved videos

Your TikTok favourites, organized automatically

Stash turns the videos you save on TikTok into a searchable personal library. You keep bookmarking as you always have, and everything quietly falls into place.

How it works

Three quiet steps. After the first one, you never have to think about it again.

1

Connect TikTok

Sign in once with TikTok. Stash asks only to read your Favourites, and you can disconnect at any time.

2

Save as usual

Bookmark any video you want to keep. That single tap is your only action, and it stays right inside TikTok.

3

Browse your library

Stash reads the videos you saved and sorts them into a tidy, searchable library you can come back to.

What Stash makes for you

Each saved video becomes something you can actually use, grouped by what it is.

Recipe cards

Cooking videos become clean recipe cards with ingredients and steps, ready when you are.

Track list

Music you saved is collected into a running track list, so a song you liked is never lost again.

How-to summaries

Coding and how-to videos become short summaries with the links you need to follow along.

Grouped by topic

Everything else is gathered into topics, so your library stays easy to search and skim.

Reads only your favourites, keeps nothing else

Your only action is bookmarking a video. Stash reads only your Favourites and discards everything else in your TikTok data the moment it arrives. Your library lives with you, and you can delete any of it whenever you like.
